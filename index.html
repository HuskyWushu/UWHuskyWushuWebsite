What is Wushu?

Wushu (武术) is a form of contemporary Chinese martial arts that blends elements of performance and martial application. Wushu training emphasizes quickness, explosive power, and natural, relaxed movement. Wushu combines flexibility with strength, speed with flawless technique, and fierce intent with effortless execution.
